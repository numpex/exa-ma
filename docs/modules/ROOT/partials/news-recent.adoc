|icon:clipboard-list[size=2x] *Dec 09, 2025*
|**Scientific Advisory Board Meeting** – Virtual (Zoom) +
Presentation of Exa-MA advances and 2025 highlights to the NumPEx Scientific Advisory Board. Topics included the 50× GenEO speedup, 8000-GPU LUMI deployment, neural operator acceleration (80-200% CPU savings), and CGAL 6.0 urban meshing achievements. Joint session with other NumPEx projects showcasing program-wide progress. Interactive session with board members providing feedback on project direction and scientific impact. +

|icon:users[size=2x] *Nov 12-14, 2025*
|**First YoungPEx General Meeting** – ENSEEIHT, Toulouse +
Three-day workshop bringing together young researchers to discuss the future of HPC and open science. Topics included sustainable computing with Georges Da Costa (Exa-SoFT), Grid'5000 tutorials, energy measurement, and workshops on social and gender equality in science. Organized by Thomas Saigre (Exa-MA), Karmijn Hoogveld (Exa-SofT), and other NumPEx members. +
