WP6: Uncertainty Quantification

WP6 quantifies uncertainties in complex multiscale simulations and propagates them through the prediction pipeline.

1. Objectives

  • Kernel-based sensitivity analysis

  • Surrogate modeling for UQ and high-dimensional integration

  • Multi-arithmetic and multi-fidelity approaches

2. Leads & Timeline

  • Lead: École Polytechnique

  • Duration: Months 1-60

3. Recent Highlights

  • Sensitivity analyses linked with WP3 solvers and WP2 surrogates

  • Initial UQ pipelines exercised on EuroHPC resources

  • Shared UQ components prepared for WP7 benchmarking

4. Next Steps

  • Expand UQ benchmarks with additional industrial test cases

  • Provide ready-to-run sensitivity examples in the showroom

  • Align UQ metrics with WP0 reporting for impact tracking
